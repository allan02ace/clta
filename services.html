Businesses need to be equipped and ready in the ever changing taxation environment which is not limited to issuances of new rules and regulations. The view is solving today the problems of tomorrow. To do this, we came up with the following taxation services:

  • Run After Tax Evaders (RATE) Cases Handling
  • Tax Compliance Review
  • Independent CPA Commissioning for Tax Cases
  • Preparation of Tax Returns
  • Computer Application System Registration

  • Tax Studies and Planning
    • Individual

    • Estate
