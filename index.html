About Us

Bridging Professional Finance, Law, and Strategy to Protect Your Business Interests.

CLTA and Co. was established on June 1, 2015 to be a one-stop shop for all your professional service needs. Whether you require Accounting, Assurance, Business Processes, Corporate and Business Law, or Taxation services, our team is equipped to deliver comprehensive solutions tailored to your business.

We consist of professionals – lawyers and certified public accountants – who joined together to serve the best interests of businesses. Our combined expertise spans multiple industries and business sizes, enabling us to understand the unique challenges you face.

We are committed to providing quality services to our clients, and we are dedicated to building long-term relationships with them. We understand that every business is unique, and we customize our approach to align with your specific goals and compliance requirements.
