About Us

Bridging Professional Finance, Law, and Strategy to Protect Your Business Interests.

CLTA and Co. was established on June 1, 2015 to be a one-stop shop for all your needs. May it be Accounting, Assurance, Business Processes, Corporate and Business Law, or Taxation, we have the expert partners and staff who can serve you.
